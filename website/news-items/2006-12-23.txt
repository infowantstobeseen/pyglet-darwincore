title=GLX support broadens
date=2006-12-23
author=richard

We now support GLX versions 1.0 and onwards (previously we only supported
1.3+). We've also got a new script "tools/info.py" which will tell you all
about your OpenGL capabilities (see a
<a href="http://www.pyglet.org/richard-info.txt">sample from my system</a>).
